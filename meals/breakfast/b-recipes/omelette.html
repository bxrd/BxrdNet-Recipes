Omelette

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetables (e.g. bell peppers, onions, mushrooms)

Instructions

  1. In a medium bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until melted and hot.
  3. Pour egg mixture into skillet and cook, stirring gently with a spatula, until eggs are set but still slightly moist.
  4. Sprinkle cheese and vegetables over half of the omelette.
  5. Using a spatula, fold the other half of the omelette over the filling.
  6. Cook for an additional minute or until cheese is melted.
  7. Slide omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
