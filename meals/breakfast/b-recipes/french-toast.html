French Toast

French toast is a classic breakfast dish that is easy to make and delicious to eat. Made with simple ingredients like bread, eggs, and milk, it's a perfect way to start your day. Whether you enjoy it plain or topped with syrup and fruit, French toast is sure to be a hit with the whole family.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Instructions

  1. In a shallow dish, whisk together eggs, milk, cinnamon, vanilla, and salt until well combined.
  2. Heat butter in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until melted and hot.
  3. Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure to coat both sides.
  4. Place bread in skillet and cook until golden brown, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Repeat with remaining slices of bread.
  6. Serve hot with syrup, fruit, or your favorite toppings.
